# RWA (Real World Assets) Outlook in Asia 2026-2029

## Executive Summary

The RWA tokenization market in Asia is transitioning from experimental pilots to active global markets in 2026. This pivotal year marks a shift from asset "minting" to sustained trading volume and secondary market liquidity. The global tokenized RWA market (excluding stablecoins) reached $19-36 billion in early 2026, with projections exceeding $100 billion by year-end. Long-term forecasts range from $400 billion to $9.43 trillion by 2030, depending on scope definitions.

---

## 1. Key Trends in Tokenization (2026-2029)

### The Pivot Year: 2026
- **From "Minted" to "Mobile"**: Industry focus shifted from creating tokens to ensuring sustained trading volume and liquidity
- **Programmable Trust**: Compliance, risk controls, and transfer restrictions embedded directly into smart contracts
- **On-Chain Delivery-versus-Payment (DvP) Settlement**: Eliminating manual reconciliation and T+2 settlement delays
- **Modular Market Structure**: Moving from vertically integrated silos to technologically unified custody, clearing, and execution
- **Tokenized Asset Mobility**: Ability for tokenized value to move across different blockchains and jurisdictions via MPC-based custody

### 2027-2029 Projections
- Standardization of RWA as repeatable, on-chain financial products
- 24/7 blockchain-based trading becoming norm (NYSE unveiled plans for 24/7 tokenized stock trading)
- Deepening liquidity through regulated hubs
- Cross-border settlement integration

## 3. Regulatory Developments by Jurisdiction

### üá≠üá∞ Hong Kong

**Key Regulator:** Securities and Futures Commission (SFC)

**2025-2026 Developments:**
- **June 2025**: New policy statement outlining RWA tokenization expansion
- **September 2025**: "A-S-P-I-Re" roadmap - exploring expansion of regulatory framework for tokenized products for professional investors
- **November 2025**: New circulars providing clarity on tokenized securities structures
- **January 2026**: Enhanced licensing regime covering VA advisors and managers

**Regulatory Framework:**
- Technology-neutral, risk-based approach under Securities and Futures Ordinance (SFO)
- **Licensing Requirements:**
  - Type 1 (Dealing in Securities)
  - Type 4 (Advising on Securities)
  - Type 7 (Automated Trading Services) - requires dual license with Type 1 for retail
  - Type 9 (Asset Management)
- **Investor Protection:** Retail vs. Professional Investors distinction; Complex Products classification
- **Custody:** Segregation of client assets, high cybersecurity standards, insurance encouraged

**Key Milestone (August 2025):** Hong Kong authorized tokenized property funds - first major "tokenized real estate as a product" in Asia

## 5. Sector Analysis

### üè¢ Real Estate

**Status:** Fastest-growing RWA segment in Asia

**Key Developments:**
- **Hong Kong (August 2025):** First authorized tokenized property funds - fractional interests in real estate projects
- **Dubai Land Department:** First real estate registration entity in Middle East to adopt blockchain-based tokenization

**Opportunities:**
- Ultra-low entry barriers to prime commercial real estate
- Stable rental income via smart contract distribution
- Global real estate exposure without geographic limits

**Challenges:**
- Legal title mapping between on-chain tokens and off-chain land registries
- Independent third-party valuations
- On-chain governance for property management decisions
